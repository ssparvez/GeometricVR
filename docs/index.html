Our Goal

To make geometry much more engaging and exciting through the power of virtual reality with the Google Cardboard. Our app is designed to provide a completely new interactive dimension to learning and visualizing three-dimensional shapes.

How It Works

There will be a grid of several three dimensional shapes you can choose from the menu screen. You can also switch between VR and non-VR views using the "cardboard" icon on the panel of buttons to the right. When a shape is chosen, a 3D representation of that shape right in the middle of the view. You can manipulate this object by zooming and rotating through the use of the VR headset trigger. Arrows on the left and right side will allow you to switch between the next and previous shapes. There will also be properties such as volume and surface area displayed at the bottom of the view about the specified 3D object.

*This app was created as a project for the Human-Computer Interaction course at Stony Brook University
